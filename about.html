OUR AIM TO SAVE NATURE

The natural environment gives us a wealth of services that are difficult to measure in dollars. Natural areas help clean our air, purify our water, produce food and medicines, reduce chemical and noise pollution, slow floodwaters, and cool our streets. We call this work 'ecosystem services'.
  • Keep our surroundings clean.
  • Plant more trees.
  • Conserve water and water bodies.
  • Educate people about the significance of conserving nature.
  • Cycle more and drive fewer cars on the road.
  • Use LED lights.
  • Minimize Food Wastage. ...
  • To ensure the protection of biodiversity.

IMPORTANCE OF NATURE

The air we breathe, the water we drink, the land we live upon, the soil we use to build our houses, and the fruit and vegetables we eat, all comes under nature. There are some untouchable things which can only be felt like the beauty of hills, the aroma of flowers, and the taste of fruits, etc.
  • Nature is our only best friend with whom we can rely upon.
  • It is our primary duty to keep our nature wealthy and healthy.
  • Nature is very precious to us.
  • It is our responsibility to balance the privilege that we are getting without disturbing it.
  • Nature provides us with fruits and vegetables to feed our hunger.
